com.drypted.quicksearch.client.core.handlers.CommandsHandler
src/client/java/com/drypted/quicksearch/client/core/handlers/CommandsHandler.java

Static facade over a CommandRegistry (REGISTRY field). Commands registered ONLY
in the static block, then rebuildCommandIndex().
- Registered (args) commands: GetPlayerHeadCommand, SaveHotbarCommand,
  SaveInventoryCommand, LoadHotbarCommand, LoadInventoryCommand,
  DeleteHotbarCommand, DeleteInventoryCommand. Load/Delete ctors throw IOException
  and are wrapped in try/catch(IOException ignored).
- getCommands(userInput, onComplete): REGISTRY.searchByUserInput(userInput).
- rebuildCommandIndex(): REGISTRY.rebuildIndex() -- call after adding/removing cmds.
- getRawCommand(name); execute(name, args, LocalPlayer) -> CommandFeedback;
  getArgSuggestions(commandName, args) -> List<String>.
Actual registry/search/exec logic lives in CommandRegistry (same package).
