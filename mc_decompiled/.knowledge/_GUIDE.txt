QuickSearch knowledge notes -- Minecraft internals
==================================================

Free-form, cross-cutting findings about Minecraft 1.21.11 that are NOT tied to a
single class (those go in .index/<version>/...). Use for patterns, pipelines,
gotchas, and version-specific quirks discovered while working.

Examples of topic files:
  rendering-pipeline.txt   - framebuffer/post-effect/shader flow gotchas
  codecs.txt               - Codec / DataResult usage patterns
  creative-tabs.txt        - tab build/rebuild lifecycle notes

RULES
  - One topic per file, named by topic (kebab-case).
  - Terse, verified facts only. Plain text. No markdown.
